Nessa used to be a Racing Greyhound until she came to us from Greyhound Rescue North East. She is 4 years old, and jet black with speckles of white.

She loves to have her ears scratched along with her neck and belly.

She just recently went on her first trip to Scotland with the family and it's safe to say she absolutely loved it.

Her racing name was Brave Black and she has not long retired with her last race being in November 2021.

She is a cuddly dog with plenty love for everyone.
